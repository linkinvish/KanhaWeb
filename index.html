About Us

Located in the vibrant city of Lucknow, Kanha Inn Hotel offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional hospitality. Our 19 well-appointed rooms provide the ideal retreat for both business and leisure travelers. Born from a passion for hospitality, Kanha Inn was created to offer weary travelers a home away from home. Whether you're visiting for business, exploring Lucknow's rich cultural heritage, or simply passing through we've designed every detail to ensure your stay is unforgettable.
